Dollars are also abstract. In a sense, money itself represents some sort of collective social value. It both allows you to buy things and is a measure of collective effort. Some of the numbers above reference money. For instance the yearly budget of St. Louis, Missouri is about $1billion. That sum is collected in taxes and fees from the people living and doing business there. That's an example of public wealth, subject to democratic oversight and deployment.
